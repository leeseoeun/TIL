**1. 서블릿 등록하기**
1. 애노테이션
2. web.xml/DD 파일
```xml
<!-- 서블릿 등록 -->
  <servlet>
    <servlet-name>s01</servlet-name>
    <servlet-class>com.eomcs.web.ex01.Servlet01</servlet-class>
  </servlet>

<!-- 서블릿을 실행할 때 사용할 URL path를 설정 -->
  <servlet-mapping>
    <servlet-name>s01</servlet-name>
    <url-pattern>/ohora/haha</url-pattern>
  </servlet-mapping>
```
